Snake Game - Classic Arcade Fun

About Snake Game

Relive the nostalgia of the classic Snake game! Navigate your snake around the grid, consume food to grow longer, and see how high you can score without hitting yourself or the boundaries.

Game Features

  • Neon Aesthetics: A modern dark-mode design with vibrant neon accents.
  • Dual Controls: Use the on-screen D-Pad or intuitive swipe gestures.
  • High Score Tracking: Challenge yourself to beat your personal best.
  • Dynamic Difficulty: The game gets more challenging as you grow longer.
