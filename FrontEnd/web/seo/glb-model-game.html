Spaceship GLB Arena - 3D GLB Flight Simulator

About Spaceship GLB Arena

Step into the cockpit of a 3D GLB starfighter in Spaceship GLB Arena. Featuring dynamic WebGL rendering via ThreeJS, roll & pitch banking physics, dual wingtip laser cannons, heat management, and deflector shield protection as you navigate deep space asteroid fields.

Game Features

  • 3D GLB Model Support: Real-time loading of 3D GLB starfighter assets.
  • Dynamic Flight Controls: Responsive WASD/Arrow steering, 360 virtual joystick, and rapid-fire laser cannons.
  • Tactical Systems: Deflector shield status, laser thermal overheating alerts, and real-time top-down radar sweep.
  • Cosmic Particle Debris: Explosive 3D particle physics on asteroid destructions and craft collisions.
