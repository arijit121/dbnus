Brick Breaker - Classic Paddle Action

About Brick Breaker

Brick Breaker brings the classic paddle-and-ball arcade experience to your fingertips. Use your reflexes to keep the ball in play and smash through rows of colorful bricks to win.

Game Features

  • Physics-Based Fun: Precise ball-to-paddle physics for a satisfying feel.
  • Glow & Particles: Modern visual effects that make every hit feel impactful.
  • Multiple Levels: Varied brick patterns and challenges to overcome.
  • Intuitive Touch Controls: Seamlessly drag the paddle to stay in the game.
