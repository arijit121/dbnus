Space Shooter - Protect the Galaxy

About Space Shooter

Embark on a mission to protect the galaxy! In Space Shooter, you must tap to destroy falling asteroids before they cross your defensive line. With increasing speed and difficulty, how long can you survive?

Game Features

  • Flame Engine Powered: High-performance physics and rendering for smooth gameplay.
  • Explosive Effects: Satisfying particle systems and glow effects.
  • Scaling Difficulty: The asteroid storm intensifies as you score higher.
  • Vibrant Space Visuals: Parallax stars and colorful cosmic entities.
