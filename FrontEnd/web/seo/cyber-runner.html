Cyber Runner - Futuristic Endless Runner

About Cyber Runner

Sprint through a neon-soaked cyberpunk metropolis! Cyber Runner tests your timing and reaction speed as you slide, jump, and weave through obstacles at ever-increasing velocity.

Game Features

  • Neon Cyber Aesthetics: Vibrant glowing cityscapes and energetic synth soundscapes.
  • Fast Reflex Gameplay: Intuitive swipe and key controls for seamless movement.
  • Energy Core Collection: Collect cyber power cores to boost your multiplier and score high.
