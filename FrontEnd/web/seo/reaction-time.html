Reaction Time - Test Your Reflexes

About Reaction Time

Find out exactly how fast your reflexes are! Reaction Time provides a simple, accurate way to measure your response speed. Tap when the screen turns green and see your results instantly.

Game Features

  • Millisecond Precision: Accurate timing using Flutter's high-performance framework.
  • Statistics Dashboard: See your last, best, and average reaction times.
  • Interactive Feedback: Visual and haptic feedback for a satisfying experience.
  • Session History: Track your progress over multiple rounds.
