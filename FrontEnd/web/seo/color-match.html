Memory Match - Train Your Brain

About Memory Match

Memory Match is a vibrant and engaging puzzle game designed to sharpen your focus and memory. Flip the cards to reveal colors and find their matching pairs in the least amount of moves.

Game Features

  • Beautiful Color Palette: Harmonious and curated colors for a premium feel.
  • Stats Tracking: Keep track of your moves, time, and best records.
  • Smooth Animations: Satisfying card-flip animations powered by Flutter.
  • Responsive Grid: Perfectly optimized for both mobile and desktop screens.
