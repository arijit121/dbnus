Tic Tac Toe - Classic X & O Battle

About Tic Tac Toe

Experience the timeless game of Tic Tac Toe with a modern, premium UI. Whether you're looking to kill a few minutes or challenge your strategic thinking, our Tic Tac Toe offers both local multiplayer and an intelligent AI to play against.

Game Features

  • Smart AI: Challenge yourself against an AI that adapts to your moves.
  • Local Multiplayer: Play with a friend on the same device.
  • Premium Design: Beautiful animations and a sleek dark mode interface.
  • Cross-Platform: Built with Flutter for a consistent experience on all devices.

How to Play

The goal is simple: be the first to get three of your marks (X or O) in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. Tap on any empty cell to place your mark.
