Ludo - Classic Board Game

About Ludo

Experience the traditional charm of Ludo with a modern, dynamic UI. Whether you're playing with family, friends, or testing your luck against our intelligent AI, Ludo provides endless entertainment and strategic gameplay.

Game Features

  • Local Multiplayer: Gather around and play with 2, 3, or 4 players on the same device.
  • Smart AI Opponents: Don't have enough players? Fill the empty spots with AI opponents that prioritize strategic moves and captures.
  • Animated Dice: Enjoy an immersive, realistic dice rolling animation.
  • Premium Design: A sleek dark-themed interface with visually clear safe spots and active player indicators.

How to Play

Roll the dice and race your four pawns from start to finish. You must roll a 6 to bring a pawn out of the home base. Capture opponent pawns to send them back, utilize safe star positions to protect your pawns, and be the first to navigate all four of your pawns into the center finish zone to win!
